## Quick Color Harmony Rules

### Complementary (High Impact)
Use primary and its opposite on the color wheel.
- Blue + Orange
- Green + Red
- Purple + Yellow

### Analogous (Harmonious)
Use colors next to each other on the wheel.
- Blue + Teal + Green
- Orange + Red + Pink
- Purple + Blue + Indigo

### Triadic (Balanced)
Use three colors equally spaced on the wheel.
- Blue + Yellow + Red
- Green + Orange + Purple

### Split-Complementary (Versatile)
Primary + two colors adjacent to its complement.
- Blue + Yellow-Orange + Red-Orange
- Green + Red-Purple + Red-Orange
